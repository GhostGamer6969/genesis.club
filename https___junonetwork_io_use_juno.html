      Non-custodial multi-chain extension wallet powered by Cosmostation, the interchain validator. Cosmostation is a non-custodial multi-chain cryptocurrency browser extension wallet that supports 50+ chains including Ethereum, Cosmos, dYdX Chain, Sui, and more. With Cosmostation, you can manage all your assets with one wallet, one key. Cosmostation has been a web3 infrastructure team contributing to more than 50 protocols since 2018. Our suite of products include Mintscan block explorer, Cosmostation non-custodial crypto wallet, and node validator service. At Cosmostation, we bootstrap networks and onboard end-users.
